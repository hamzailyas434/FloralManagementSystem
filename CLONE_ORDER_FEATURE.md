### **2. Cloning Process**
1. Click **Clone** button on an order.
2. Confirm the action in the popup.
3. Redirects to **New Order** page.
4. **Pre-fills** the form with data from the original order:
   - Customer Details
   - Order Items (Products, Fabrics, etc.)
   - Delivery Cost settings
   - Order Type
   - Notes
5. **Resets** specific fields for the new order:
   - **Order Number:** Generates a new one
   - **Date:** Sets to today
   - **Status:** Resets to 'Received'
   - **Payment:** Resets to 'Remaining' (Paid: 0)
   - **Tracking ID:** Cleared
